Camel

  • Camel's Ears Are Furry
  • Camels have thick eyelashes to protect their eyes from the Desert dust
  • Camels can endure up to two full weeks without drinking water.

Chameleons

  • Chameleons have eyes that can swivel.
  • Sick chameleons look pale in color.
  • Chameleons mostly live in Africa, Asia, and southern Europe.

Pigeons

  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
  • Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet, and at an average speed of 77.6 mph.

Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • The world's smallest stitched teddy bear is a mere 0.29 inches tall.
